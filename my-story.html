My Story – The Girl Behind the Dreams ♡

I'm a 19-year-old second-year student at EPI Digital School, constantly discovering who I am beyond the achievements. Growing up, I feared invisibility, so I pursued excellence relentlessly — top grades, grand dreams.

My father's career as a worldwide-published writer took our family to the Gulf for years, immersing me in a new culture. Belonging everywhere and nowhere reshaped my worldview, enriched by diverse educational systems and thirteen summers between deserts and skyscrapers.

That tender-hearted girl who dreamed of healing as a doctor never left — she shines through my national piano wins, international writing awards, CoPro leadership, and my current project aiding children with cancer.

One day, she'll lead Google as CEO, drive global UN initiatives touching millions, and grace legendary halls as one of the greatest concert pianists — turning emotion into music on 88 keys.

She was always meant for this.
And she’s only just beginning.
